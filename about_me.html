    • About Me

    Hi there! I'm Gil.

    I'm a fourth-year Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) student at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD). So far, it's been a blast! I've really enjoyed diving into computer science and exploring its many disciplines (Computer Architecture, Algorithms, Software Engineering, Operating Systems, Networking, Web Development, etc.).

    I particularly like going into the low-level aspects of computer science to understand how computers work and how to build my own, as well as to have an understanding of the different "layers" of computer science; this is the main inspiration behind the ByteFrost project, a project in which my dad and I have been building a homebrewed 8-bit breadboard CPU.

    I also love classical music and perform and compose my own music. I've been playing the piano for over ten years now, and I've enjoyed it a lot. Since my music is an important aspect of who I am, I've incorporated it into this website; if you have a moment and want to have a listen to some of my work, you can check out my latest piece.
