    Project Description

    Wrath of Zeus is a 3v1 asymmetric maze escape games where 3 players attempt to escape a maze while Zeus tries to stop them from doing so. It was developed from scratch over the course of 10 weeks as a project for UCSD's CSE 125 course run by Professor Geoffrey Voelker.

    I contributed to the design and implementation of the game state representation and server-side logic, ObjectManager implementation, GridCell maze map representation implementation, O(n) collision detection optimization, the design of the game, the lobby system (state machine and table UI), the Orb and Mirror items, and other utilities.

    I also wrote the entire soundtrack of the game!
